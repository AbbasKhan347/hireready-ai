FAQ
Frequently asked questions
How good is the AI-generated cover letter?
Very good! Our AI writes professional, ATS-optimised cover letters that match UK and US hiring standards. The more details you provide — especially the job description — the more tailored your letter will be. Over 94% of our users report getting interview callbacks.
What is ATS and why does it matter?
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most large companies use ATS software to automatically filter cover letters before a human ever reads them. Our letters are specifically written to pass ATS filters by including the right keywords and formatting.
Can I edit my cover letter after generating it?
Yes — with Pro. Pro users can click directly on any part of the letter and edit it inside the app. Once happy, download as PDF or TXT. Free users can see the first half only.
Is my data safe and private?
Yes. We do not store your personal information or the content you enter. Each generation is processed securely and deleted immediately. We never share your data with third parties.
Is it a one-time payment or subscription?
Absolutely. It is a one-time payment — no subscription, no recurring charges, no cancellation needed. You pay once and get cover letters according to you plan. Simple as that.
Does it work for any country or job type?
Yes! HireReady AI works for any job in any country. Optimised for great results for any market worldwide.
Can I use this for the Resume Summary without uploading a PDF?
Yes. The Resume Summary feature is text-based — just type or paste your experience, skills, and background. No file upload required. The AI generates a professional summary paragraph you can use at the top of your resume. This feature is available on the Pro plan.
