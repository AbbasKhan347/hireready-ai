FAQ
Frequently asked questions
Is HireReady AI really free?
Yes! HireReady AI is 100% free for students and job seekers. No credit card, no limits, no hidden fees. Generate unlimited cover letters and resumes.
How does the Resume Builder work?
Enter your job title, experience, skills, and optionally a job description. Our AI will generate a professional, ATS‑optimised resume with quantified bullet points and role‑specific keywords. You can then copy or download as PDF.
How good is the AI‑generated resume?
Very good! Our AI writes clear, achievement‑oriented bullet points that highlight your impact. We use the same powerful AI as our cover letter generator, tailored specifically for resume writing.
Can I download my resume as PDF?
Yes! After generating your resume, click "Download PDF" to get a clean, professionally formatted PDF file ready to attach to your applications.
What is ATS and why does it matter?
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software that filters resumes before a human sees them. Our tool automatically includes relevant keywords and formatting that help your resume pass these filters.
Is my data safe and private?
Yes. We do not store your personal information or the content you enter. Each generation is processed securely and deleted immediately. We never share your data with third parties.
Does it work for any job or country?
Yes! HireReady AI works for any job in any country. Optimised for US/UK standards but produces great results worldwide.
