Yes! HireReady AI is 100% free for students and job seekers. No credit card required, no limits, no hidden fees. Generate as many cover letters as you need.
How good is the AI-generated cover letter?
Very good! Our AI writes professional, ATS-optimised cover letters that match UK and US hiring standards. The more details you provide — especially the job description — the more tailored your letter will be. Students consistently report getting interview callbacks after using our letters.
What is ATS and why does it matter?
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Most large companies use ATS software to automatically filter cover letters before a human ever reads them. Our letters are specifically written to pass ATS filters by including the right keywords and formatting.
Can I edit my cover letter after generating it?
Yes — just click the copy button, paste it into Google Docs or Word, and make any changes you like. It is your letter and you are in full control.
Can I download my cover letter as PDF?
Yes! After generating your cover letter click the Download PDF button and it saves a clean, professional PDF file directly to your device — ready to attach to any job application.

Yes. We do not store your personal information or the content you enter. Each generation is processed securely and deleted immediately. We never share your data with third parties.

Yes! HireReady AI works for any job in any country. Optimised for US and UK standards but produces great results for any market worldwide including Europe, Australia, Canada, and more.
